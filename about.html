About Excell Equipment

Excell Equipment is organized to engage in the business of selling, distributing, and brokering power equipment and related products, including but not limited to industrial, commercial, and consumer-grade machinery. We may also provide associated services such as repairs, maintenance, warranty support, leasing, and consulting to ensure that our customers receive comprehensive care for their equipment investments.

In furtherance of this purpose, Excell Equipment may perform any and all acts and exercise any and all powers necessary, convenient, or incidental to conducting the above activities in the State of Michigan, in accordance with applicable laws. Our team draws on decades of combined experience in mechanical engineering, sales, and customer support to deliver reliable solutions to businesses of all sizes.

Our Mission

To provide dependable, high-quality power equipment solutions that empower our clients to operate efficiently and safely, while delivering exceptional customer service and ongoing support.
